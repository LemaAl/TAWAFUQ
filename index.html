the problem

Most public speaking tools unfairly penalize any negative facial expression, even when the topic is serious or emotional. They evaluate face and speech separately — missing the full picture.

our solution

Tawafuq analyzes facial expressions, voice, and speech together — judging performance based on emotional alignment, not raw expressions alone. Fair, accurate, actionable.

project objectives
01Design and implement an AI system to evaluate public speaking performance
02Develop algorithms that align facial expressions with speech sentiment to avoid unfair scoring
03Provide actionable feedback on key delivery features including voice and expressions
04Create a user-friendly interface that lets individuals easily understand their feedback
05Test and evaluate the system's effectiveness in improving public speaking skills
